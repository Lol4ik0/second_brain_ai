Neural Networks

Last edited: 2 hours ago

Introduction

Neural networks are the foundation of modern deep learning systems. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes that process information in a way inspired by biological neurons.

Architecture

A typical neural network consists of:

  • Input Layer - receives the raw data
  • Hidden Layers - process and transform the data
  • Output Layer - produces the final prediction
