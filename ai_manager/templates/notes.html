Neural Networks

Last edited: 2 hours ago

Introduction

Neural networks are the foundation of modern deep learning systems. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes that process information in a way inspired by biological neurons.

Architecture

A typical neural network consists of the following components:

  • Input Layer - receives the raw, unformatted data.
  • Hidden Layers - processes and transforms the data using weights and biases.
  • Output Layer - produces the final probability or prediction.
